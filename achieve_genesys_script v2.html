Objection handling
Reference guide
►
"I need to think about it." ►
That's completely fair. Can I ask — is there a specific part you'd like more time to think through? I want to make sure you have everything you need to feel confident. Many of our members felt the same way, and once we walked through the numbers together, they felt much more at ease.
"I'm worried about my credit score." ►
That's one of the most common concerns we hear, and it's a great question. While debt resolution can have a temporary impact, carrying high unresolved debt can also damage your score long-term. Our goal is to get you to a healthier financial position overall — let me share what that typically looks like for members in a similar situation.
"I already tried another company." ►
I appreciate you sharing that — it means you're serious about tackling this. What I can tell you is that Achieve is different. We've helped over [X] members and have been doing this for over 20 years. Can you tell me a bit about what didn't work before? That helps me make sure we set the right expectations this time.
"How do I know this is legitimate?" ►
That's a smart question and I'm glad you asked. Achieve is accredited by the American Fair Credit Council and the International Association of Professional Debt Arbitrators. We're fully licensed and have maintained an A+ rating. Would you like me to point you to where you can verify this independently?
